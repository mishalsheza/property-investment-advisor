Source: Zoning and FSI/FAR Primer (illustrative regulatory reference)

Indian municipal development plans classify land into zones (residential, commercial,
industrial, mixed-use, no-development/green zones). Floor Space Index (FSI), also called
Floor Area Ratio (FAR), caps the built-up area relative to plot size and varies by city,
zone, and proximity to metro corridors (many cities grant FSI premiums near transit
nodes, e.g., Transit-Oriented Development bonuses near metro stations in Bangalore,
Mumbai, and Pune). Buyers of plots or under-construction property should confirm zoning
classification and FSI eligibility with the local municipal corporation, since
unauthorized construction beyond sanctioned FSI is subject to demolition or regularization
penalties under state unauthorized-construction regulations.
