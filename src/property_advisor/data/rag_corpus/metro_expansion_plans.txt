Source: Metro & Infrastructure Expansion Tracker (illustrative dataset for this capstone)

Bangalore: Namma Metro Purple Line extension into Whitefield is operational, materially
improving connectivity to the ORR/Whitefield IT corridor and supporting residential
demand in Whitefield, Hoodi, and surrounding localities.

Mumbai: Metro Line 3 (Colaba-Bandra-SEEPZ, underground) is under phased construction and
expected to improve connectivity for Worli and South Mumbai over the medium term. Metro
Line 2B improves connectivity along the Dadar-Kurla-Andheri corridor, though this does
not mitigate the area's existing monsoon flood-risk classification.

Pune: A proposed metro extension into Hinjewadi Phase 2/3 has faced repeated timeline
delays due to right-of-way and funding disputes; as of the latest tracked update no firm
revised completion date has been confirmed, which is a key source of analyst disagreement
on the corridor's medium-term appreciation outlook.
